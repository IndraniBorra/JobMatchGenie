    Job Match Genie

    🧠 Welcome to Job Match Genie!

    Job Match Genie is an intelligent job application assistant that simplifies your job search process with three powerful tools:

    • 📄 Resume Parser: Upload your resume and extract key information like email, phone number, skills, and a clean text preview using AI-powered parsing.
    • 📊 Resume Score: Upload both your resume and a job description to get a match score that evaluates how well your resume aligns with the job.
    • ✉️ Cover Letter Generator: Generate a personalized, professional cover letter instantly based on your resume and the job description.

    Whether you're applying for your dream job or fine-tuning your resume, Job Match Genie makes the process faster, smarter, and easier.
